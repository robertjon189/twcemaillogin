Configuring TWC Email in Email Clients

IMAP Configuration Settings

To set up your TWC email in desktop or mobile email clients, use the following IMAP settings: Incoming server: mail.twc.com, Port: 993 (SSL/TLS), Outgoing server: mail.twc.com, Port: 587 (STARTTLS). These settings allow you to access your email through applications like Outlook, Apple Mail, or mobile email apps.

POP3 Alternative Settings

If you prefer POP3 configuration, use: Incoming server: mail.twc.com, Port: 995 (SSL), Outgoing server: mail.twc.com, Port: 587 (STARTTLS). POP3 downloads emails to your device, while IMAP keeps them synchronized across all devices accessing your account.

Mobile Device Setup

Most mobile devices can automatically configure TWC email settings when you provide your email address and password. If automatic configuration fails, manually enter the server settings mentioned above in your device's email setup process.

Configuration Note: Always use secure connection settings (SSL/TLS) when configuring TWC email in third-party applications to protect your login credentials and email content from potential security threats.

Best Practices for TWC Email Security

Create a unique, strong password for your TWC email account using a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using personal information or common words that could be easily guessed by malicious actors attempting to access your account.

Regularly check your email account for any suspicious activity, including unfamiliar sent messages, unexpected password change notifications, or login alerts from unknown locations. Report any suspicious activity to twc email customer service immediately.
